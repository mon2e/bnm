Broke No More (BNM) - Your Path to Financial Freedom

About Us

BNM is a youth investment group that empowers individuals through smart investments. Membership is based on financial contributions, with shares proportional to investments.

How It Works

  • Contribute funds and become a shareholder.
  • Investments are made in vetted opportunities.
  • Profits are shared based on contribution percentages.
  • Members have a say in investment decisions.
